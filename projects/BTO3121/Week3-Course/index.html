Ecem's Notes:The World’s Best City: İstanbul

İstanbul is one of the most fascinating cities in the world, where history and modern life blend perfectly. It is the only city in the world that sits on two continents — Europe and Asia — separated by the beautiful Bosphorus Strait. With its rich cultural heritage, delicious food, and stunning architecture, İstanbul truly deserves to be called one of the world’s best cities.

    Top 5 Reasons Why İstanbul Is Among the Best Cities :
  1. Its unique location between Europe and Asia.
  2. Incredible historical landmarks such as Hagia Sophia and Topkapı Palace.
  3. The diverse and flavorful Turkish cuisine.
  4. The warm and welcoming local people.
  5. The lively atmosphere of its markets, neighborhoods, and nightlife.
    Other Things You Shouldn’t Miss in İstanbul:
  1. A walk along the Bosphorus.
  2. Tasting traditional dishes like kebab, meze, and baklava.
  3. Visiting the Grand Bazaar and Spice Market.
  4. Watching the sunset from Galata Tower.
  5. Taking a ferry ride between the two continents.
    Learn More About İstanbul:
  • Visit İstanbul Official Tourism Site
  • Go Türkiye – İstanbul
  • UNESCO World Heritage: Historic Areas of İstanbul

Conclusion

İstanbul is more than just a travel destination — it is a living museum of civilizations, a bridge between cultures, and a city that never loses its charm. Whether you are walking through its ancient streets or enjoying a cup of Turkish tea by the Bosphorus, İstanbul leaves a lasting impression on everyone who visits.
